Biography

MI6’s top spy in America, codenamed ‘Intrepid,’ Churchill’s chief American intelligence operative and the inspiration for Ian Fleming’s James Bond. Led the mission with Wild Bill Donovan (OSS) that had Brown parachuted into Nazi Germany in 1945. Sent a telegram from the Rockefeller Plaza building to Brown’s wife: ‘Your husband is recuperating fine in an English hospital. We’ll send him home soon.’ Nassau was his base of operations; he met with the Caroline Group aboard Eldridge Reeves Johnson’s yacht.
