Biography

Iranian-American philosopher and author. Born in New York to Iranian parents. His work develops a unified philosophical framework spanning parapsychology, UAP phenomenology, Nazi black programmes, political theory, and Iranian civilizational history.

Books include Prometheus and Atlas (2016), Iranian Leviathan (2019), Closer Encounters (2021), and World State of Emergency (2017). He describes himself as following in the tradition of real philosophers — those who question without exception across ontology, epistemology, ethics, politics, and aesthetics.

His UAP framework argues that a Nordic hyperdimensional civilization engineered humanity, seeded every major religion as a control mechanism, and maintains continuous influence through the deep state and occult brotherhoods. His Nazi breakaway civilisation thesis holds that the SS-derived fascist elite co-constituted the CIA through the Gehlen Organisation and is executing a century-long social engineering programme (1945–2045) toward a thousand-year imperium.
