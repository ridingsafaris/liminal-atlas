Biography

Garry Nolan is one of the most credentialed scientists to publicly engage with UAP research. A tenured professor at Stanford School of Medicine, he holds the Rachford & Carlota A. Harris endowed chair in the Department of Pathology. His academic career spans cancer biology, autoimmunity, and systems immunology, producing over 300 research articles and 40+ US patents. He has been named one of Stanford’s top 25 inventors and has founded eight biotech companies, several of which were acquired for nine-figure sums.

His entry into UAP research began when the CIA approached him unsolicited around 2019, asking him to analyse MRI scans of DoD and intelligence personnel who had reported UAP encounters. The agency had selected him specifically because he had built the most capable single-cell analysis tools then available (CyTOF, CODEX). Through this work he was introduced to what insiders call the Invisible College — a network including Jacques Vallée, Hal Puthoff, Eric Davis, Robert Bigelow, and Colm Kelleher.

Nolan found a consistent neurological signature: increased neural density in the caudate–putamen region of the basal ganglia, present in UAP contactees and their family members, suggesting a genetic rather than acquired origin. He theorises this structure widens perceptual filters, allowing individuals to perceive phenomena that others suppress or cannot register.

For physical materials, he works with the archive assembled by Jacques Vallée — fragments sent by crash witnesses worldwide — analysing them using Stanford’s NanoSIMS (secondary ion mass spectrometer). The most notable sample from Ubá Tuba, Brazil showed magnesium isotope ratios far outside natural terrestrial range. A bismuth–magnesium layered sample allegedly from Roswell was described by Hal Puthoff as having the structural property of miniaturising terahertz microwave guides by a factor of ~30.

In 2023 he co-founded The Sol Foundation with sociocultural anthropologist Dr. Peter Skafish — a Stanford-linked nonprofit conducting academic research into the scientific, policy, and philosophical implications of UAP. David Grusch served as its COO. He has publicly stated 100% probability that non-human intelligence has visited Earth, has appeared before Congress informally, and has vouched for whistleblower David Grusch.
