Biography

Physicist who led the University of Colorado’s contracted UFO study for the USAF (1966–69). His final report, the so-called Condon Report, recommended termination of Project Blue Book. The Australian JIO file’s Turner analysis is notably critical: “the conclusions of the Condon report conflict with its own contents and has been discredited by many reputable scientists including the UFO scientific consultant to the USAF.” Condon had publicly stated his negative conclusion before completing the study — a fact that led to staff dismissals and resignations. The Condon Report is the official justification cited for Blue Book’s termination.
