Biography

Physicist who chaired the CIA’s Office of Scientific Intelligence panel convened in January 1953 to recommend policy on UFOs. The Robertson Panel — which included Luis Alvarez, Lloyd Berkner, Samuel Goudsmit, and Thornton Page — recommended a “debunking” campaign lasting 1–2 years to reduce public interest in UFOs. The Australian JIO file’s Turner analysis treats this as the key evidence of deliberate US suppression. The Robertson Report was sanitised twice (1958, 1966) before full declassification. Its conclusions directly led to Project Blue Book being gutted from 10 top-secret staff to one airman by September 1953.
