Biography

Britain’s most prominent aviation journalist, long-time Jane’s Defence Weekly contributor. Author of The Hunt for Zero Point (2001), which documents decades of conversations with physicists, aerospace executives, and multiple Skunk Works presidents on the history of anti-gravity research. His pseudonymous source ‘Dan Marcus’ provided key information on Northrop’s classified electroaerodynamics work and the B2 stealth bomber’s alleged Biefeld-Brown implementation. Confirmed to Jesse, in an off-camera meeting with Paul Shatkin, that their independent sources both pointed to the B2 using aspects of Brown’s principles.
