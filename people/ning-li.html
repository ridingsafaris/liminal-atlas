Biography

Chinese-American physicist who worked at the University of Alabama in Huntsville and published peer-reviewed papers in the 1990s on theoretical mechanisms for gravitational shielding using rotating superconductors. Her 1989 paper (co-authored with Douglas Torr) described how a rotating superconductor might generate a detectable gravitational field. She reportedly received US Army research funding for continued work, then disappeared from public view in the early 2000s. Dave Rossi describes her research as one of the key documented instances of anti-gravity work pursued within mainstream academia before being suppressed or classified.
