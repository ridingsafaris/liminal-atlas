Biography

Former Assistant Secretary of Housing and Urban Development under President George H.W. Bush and former managing director of Dillon, Read & Co. Spent 11 years in DOJ litigation after attempting to stop HUD mortgage fraud. Founder of the Solari Report. Working with economist Mark Skidmore (Michigan State), documented $21 trillion in undocumentable federal budget adjustments between 1998–2015. Her frameworks — the central banking warfare model, the financial coup d’état, Mr. Global, and the breakaway civilisation — are the intellectual architecture of the Danny Jones interview close reading. Also cited by Jesse Michels on JRE #2331.
