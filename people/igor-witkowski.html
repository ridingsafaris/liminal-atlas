Biography

Polish journalist and military historian who conducted field work at the Schoto Works site outside Prague. First published the Die Glocke accounts based on SS officer Jacob Sporrenberg's post-war testimony under interrogation by Polish prosecutors. His field investigation confirmed physical evidence of the underground facility.

Nick Cook's The Hunt for Zero Point and Jorjani's Closer Encounters both rely heavily on Witkowski's research. Key books: The Truth About the Wunderwaffe.
