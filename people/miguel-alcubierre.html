Biography

Mexican theoretical physicist at UNAM. In 1994 proposed the Alcubierre metric — a solution to Einstein’s field equations permitting faster-than-light travel by contracting spacetime ahead of a vessel and expanding it behind, without the vessel exceeding c locally. Requires negative energy density, whose physical existence remains unconfirmed. Discussed by Eric Hazelton as within the “not impossible” propulsion framework.
