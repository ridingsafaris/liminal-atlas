Biography

Jesse Michels is the creator and host of American Alchemy — one of the most research-intensive interview series in the UAP and frontier science space. He is CEO of American Alchemy Holdings and manages a venture portfolio on behalf of Peter Thiel’s family office (Thiel Capital), where his career began and where he first encountered many of the researchers he would later interview.

He grew up in Santa Monica. His father is Barry Michels, therapist and co-author of The Tools. He attended Columbia University as a Jon Jay Scholar, graduating with a BA in History. At Columbia he interned for The Daily Show with Jon Stewart and Charlie Rose. He then worked in AI Operations at Google, also producing the “Talks at Google” interview series.

At Thiel Capital in LA he engaged in traditional venture investing and organised intellectual events connecting thinkers across religion, politics, economics, and unconventional science — including hosting Erich von Däniken. This exposure shifted his focus toward UAP, frontier physics, and suppressed science history. American Alchemy launched as a YouTube channel dedicated to “the most heretical thinkers and ideas of our time.”

Jesse produced one of the first major interviews with David Grusch (years before Grusch’s congressional testimony) and what may be the most technically detailed open-source treatment of the Townsend Brown anti-gravity tech tree. The deputy CTO of Northrop Grumman has reportedly recommended the video to investors as “actionable stuff.” He appeared on The Joe Rogan Experience #2331 in 2025.

Jesse has personally examined the M-type Nazca specimens in Peru — the tridactyl mummified entities found in 2015 — and is producing a full documentary on the case.

Key Research Contributions

The Townsend Brown Tech Tree — Jesse’s most cited work. A documented chain from Brown’s 1923 vacuum thrust experiments through the declassified 1971 Australian JIO document (naming Oppenheimer, Teller, Dyson, Wheeler in anti-gravity research), the 1957 Chapel Hill conference, Northrop’s 1968 electroaerodynamics paper, Aviation Week’s 1992 “surfs its own wave” claim, NASA Kennedy’s Charles Buhler attributing his electrostatics work to Brown, and the magnesium-bismuth link between Brown’s 1954 Winterhaven proposal and UAP crash material analysed by Gary Nolan at Stanford.

David Grusch Pre-Testimony Interview — One of the first substantive on-camera interviews with the UAP whistleblower, conducted before his congressional emergence. Gave the American Alchemy audience significant early access to Grusch’s claims about crash retrieval programmes.

Nazca Mummy Investigation — Among the first Western investigators to personally examine the M-type specimens. His coverage balances genuine forensic evidence (multi-expert consensus on real anatomy, CAT-scanned pregnant specimen) with honest acknowledgement of the contaminated DNA signal and the J-type hoax problem.

Synthesis platform — American Alchemy has become the primary venue for researchers across the UAP-consciousness-frontier-physics nexus who are too heterodox for mainstream media but too serious for most UFO YouTube. Guests include Jacques Vallée, Hal Puthoff, Gary Nolan, Eric Weinstein, Avi Loeb, Diana Pasulka, Ryan Graves, and Jason Reza Jorjani.
