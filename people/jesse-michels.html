Biography

Jesse Michels is the creator and host of American Alchemy — one of the most research-intensive interview series in the UAP and frontier science space. He is CEO of American Alchemy Holdings and manages a venture portfolio on behalf of Peter Thiel’s family office (Thiel Capital), where his career began and where he first encountered many of the researchers he would later interview.

He grew up in Santa Monica. His father is Barry Michels, therapist and co-author of The Tools. He attended Columbia University as a Jon Jay Scholar, graduating with a BA in History. At Columbia he interned for The Daily Show with Jon Stewart and Charlie Rose. He then worked in AI Operations at Google, also producing the “Talks at Google” interview series. At Thiel Capital he engaged in traditional venture investing and organised intellectual events, including hosting Erich von Däniken.

American Alchemy launched as a YouTube channel dedicated to “the most heretical thinkers and ideas of our time.” His guests include Jason Reza Jorjani, Dave Rossi, Sal Pais, David Grusch, Jacques Vallée, Hal Puthoff, Gary Nolan, Eric Weinstein, Avi Loeb, Diana Pasulka, and Ryan Graves, among others.

He has personally examined the M-type Nazca specimens in Peru — the tridactyl mummified entities discovered in 2015. His treatment of the Townsend Brown anti-gravity tech tree — tracing the lineage from Brown’s 1923 experiments through the 1971 Australian JIO document, the Chapel Hill conference, and into the B2 stealth bomber — has been cited by the deputy CTO of Northrop Grumman as “actionable stuff” for investors.
