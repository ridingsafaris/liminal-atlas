Chapter Summary

Key evidence: (1) The 1971 Australian Joint Intelligence Organisation document (verified, cited by David Grusch) naming Oppenheimer, Freeman Dyson, John Wheeler and Edward Teller as participants in secret anti-gravity research, with the CIA’s Office of Scientific Intelligence coordinating everything. But Jesse links it to Jesse Marcel’s 1947 Roswell accounts of a “memory metal” foil-like material that reformed when crumpled — and to metallurgist Elroy John Center, FOIAed in 2012 as a co-author on that 1949 Battelle paper, who told MUFON researchers before his death that he worked on alien material with hieroglyphics. Jesse argues Brown’s work fed into Northrop’s classified programmes, and possibly into the TR-3A/B triangular series. The B2 remains classified at a higher level than any allied-nation sale — no B2 is sold even to Norway or Canada.

The Nazca Mummies: Three-Fingered Tridactyls, Forensic Experts & the DNA Problem

📋
Chapter Summary

The episode’s final major topic and arguably its most contentious. Jesse describes the Nazca mummy case: small mummified entities found in a cave in Peru in 2015 by a local man named Leandro, preserved in diatomaceous earth (a desiccant — meaning they may not technically be mummies, as their organs are still intact). Three types: S types (small winged creatures), J types (the 25–30 Close-Encounters-looking ones presented at the Mexican Congress in September 2023), and M types (8–10 four-to-five-foot specimens). Jesse has seen the M types in person. All have three fingers and three toes (tridactyl). One specimen (Montserrat) is pregnant with what appears via CAT scan to be a tridactyl foetus inside. They have osmium and cadmium implants — rare earth metals discovered in the 19th century. Forensic experts who have examined them include: John McAfee Award winner Dr. McDow, Denver chief medical examiner Jim Caruso, Peruvian forensics head Dr. David Ruiz, and Mexican Navy forensics chief Dr. Jose Salce. The complication: artist Manuel Caceres was arrested at a Peruvian airport with fake versions he had constructed, and Reuters reported the case as definitively fake. The DNA testing is inconclusive — low signal-to-noise ratio from suspected human contamination. Jesse thinks the M types remain genuinely unknown and that the forensic expert consensus (real anatomy, real organs, real bones, real cartilage) makes the hoax hypothesis very hard to maintain. He links the tridactyl cave art from the Nazca period (textiles showing three-fingered three-toed beings with large heads) to the Bob Lazar craft interior detail — the Sport Model had hand-sized indentations apparently designed for three-fingered beings of about three to four feet tall.

Thomas Townsend Brown
Inventor · Biefeld-Brown Effect · 1905–1985
Ohio-born electrical engineer and inventor who in the early 1920s discovered what he called electrogravitic thrust — movement produced by a high-voltage asymmetric capacitor. Studied under Paul Biefield at Denison University, then Robert Millikan at Caltech. Demonstrated thrust in vacuum chambers in Paris (1955) and at Bahnson Labs NC (1958). Funded in the 1960s by Floyd Odlum (Northrop majority owner) at Guidance Technologies, Santa Monica. Associated with Bill Lear, Edward Teller, and Robert Sarbacher. His Winterhaven proposal (1954) described electrogravitic propulsion using magnesium-bismuth as the high-K dielectric.

Bob Lazar
S4 Physicist · Area 51 Whistleblower · Element 115
Self-described physicist who in 1989 claimed to have worked at a classified facility (S4) near Area 51 reverse-engineering extraterrestrial craft. Described a ‘sport model’ disc powered by a gravity wave amplifier using Element 115 (moscovium) as fuel. His educational credentials were erased from MIT and Caltech records. He also describes the craft’s interior as designed for beings approximately three to four feet tall with three-fingered hands — consistent with the Nazca mummy descriptions. His FBI lab was raided during Jeremy Corbell’s documentary, reportedly for a sample of Element 115.

John Lear
CIA Cargo Pilot · UFO Researcher · Son of Lear Jet Inventor
Son of Bill Lear (inventor of the Lear Jet and the autopilot, associate of Townsend Brown). Flew cargo jets for CIA-connected operations. Security guards at Area 51 knew him by name — he had camped near the base for years. Claims to have been used as a deliberate ‘blabbermouth’ by Admiral Mike McClendon to get the Bob Lazar story into the public, as a limited hangout to gauge public reaction. Also promoted claims about bases on the moon and a soul-catcher device. His connection to G. Gordon Liddy (of Watergate fame) is documented photographically.

Gary Nolan
Stanford Tenured Professor · Immunologist · UAP Materials Analyst
Professor at Stanford School of Medicine, founder of multiple biotechnology companies worth over $100M each. Runs the Sol Foundation studying non-human intelligence. Has received alleged UAP crash material samples from Jacques Vallée and others, including magnesium-bismuth specimens. His analysis has found unusual isotopic ratios in these samples that he cannot explain by conventional manufacturing. Co-authored peer-reviewed papers on UAP-related phenomena. Jesse Michels references him as one of the most credentialled scientists currently engaged with the material evidence question.

John Brandenberg
Plasma Physicist · Sandia / Lawrence Livermore · Mars Nuclear Holocaust
PhD plasma physicist who worked at Sandia National Labs and Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory. Proposed the ‘Xenon-129 hypothesis’ for Mars: that the isotopic abundance of Xenon-129 and Argon-40 in the Martian atmosphere is significantly above what normal radioactive decay would produce, and that the only explanation consistent with the data is a thermonuclear detonation of planetary scale. Published in peer-reviewed form. Jesse Michels notes he was interviewed on American Alchemy and that his credentials make the claim worth examining even if the conclusion is extraordinary.

Joe McMoneagle
CIA Remote Viewer · Stargate Programme · Remote Viewer #001
Considered the most decorated remote viewer in the US Army’s Stargate programme. Reportedly tasked by the CIA in the 1990s (declassified) with remote viewing Mars ‘one million BC’, during which he described ruins and humanoid beings 12–14 feet tall around pyramidal structures. The session is in the declassified records. Author of Mind Trek and Remote Viewing Secrets. Also involved in remote viewing lunar structures.

Jaime Maussan
Mexican Journalist · UFO Researcher · Nazca Mummy Presenter
Former 60 Minutes correspondent in Mexico, now one of the most prominent UAP researchers in Latin America. In September 2023 presented two J-type Nazca specimens before the Mexican Congress, attracting global attention and significant controversy. Has invested significant resources in protecting and studying the Nazca specimens. His approach — going public with incomplete evidence — is criticised by some researchers but defended as necessary to force scientific engagement. Jesse Michels describes him as “awesome” despite reservations about the J-types specifically.
