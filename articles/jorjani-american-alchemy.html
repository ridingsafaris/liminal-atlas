Who is Jason Reza Jorjani?

Jason Reza Jorjani is an Iranian-American philosopher (Ph.D., Stony Brook) who taught at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. He calls the political-epistemic overthrow of this structure the spectral revolution.

The theoretical principle, demonstrated experimentally in 2012, that erasing one bit of information releases a minimum amount of energy (kT ln2). Jorjani uses this to argue that data has mass (dark matter) and that exponential data growth will eventually cause a geophysical catastrophe.
